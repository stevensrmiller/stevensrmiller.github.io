PushButton.UpdateListener Method

See Also

Your subclass of GadgetObserver must implement this method to be notified whenever the Gadget you observe has its Interact method called. Note that the "isLatched" parameter is passed here as a reminder that, if a Gadget has its "stayCompleted" flag set true, and has ever been completed, you most likely don't want to update your observer in the normal way. More likely, you will want to present the human player with some indication that the this Gadget is no longer interactive.
